💬 Latin Music Buzz:

Bad Bunny Unplugged, Rauw’s Island Love, and Miko + Tini Trouble?

By The Latin Sound Hub • April 16, 2025

It’s been a *week* in the Latin music world, and the streets are loud with talk—from acoustic vibes to heartbreak rumors. Here's what everyone's whispering (and tweeting) about:

🎙️ Bad Bunny Gets Intimate at Tiny Desk

Yes, you read that right—Bad Bunny pulled up to NPR's Tiny Desk for a surprise session that’s already giving fans goosebumps. Stripped of pyrotechnics and flashing lights, Benito reminded us that he's not just a performer—he’s a storyteller. Highlights included raw versions of “LO QUE LE PASO A HAWAii” and “PIToRRO DE COCO”, plus a soulful shoutout to Boricua culture. Internet? Broken. Again.

🏝️ Rauw Alejandro Drops “Carita Linda” as a Love Letter to Puerto Rico

Just when we thought Rauw couldn’t get more romantic, he released “Carita Linda”, and y’all—it’s basically a sonic hug to the island. Bringing back original puertorican sounds, Rauw makes una bomba. The track pays tribute to Puerto Rican culture, neighborhoods, and that unique island warmth. The music video? Traditional dances, beautiful faces, the ocean and sun. 10/10 cultural reset.

💔 Trouble in Paradise for Young Miko & Tini?

Now for the chisme: Rumors are swirling that rising trap queen Young Miko and pop icon Tini may have quietly called it quits. Fans noticed some suspicious Instagram unfollows and a lack of recent photos, not to mention Tini’s cryptic lyric snippet posted on her story: “Lo que duele, también enseña.” Um... okay, therapist 😅

No confirmation from either camp yet, but let’s just say Twitter is already in heartbreak mode. We'll be watching this story closely, besties.
