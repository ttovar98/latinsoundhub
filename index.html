Highlights!

The band "Rawayana" made their first appearance this last weekend at Coachella, dedicating their performance to their fellow Venezuelans following the recent turmoil the country has expecienced, followed by the persecution in several other countries, the lead singer Beto Montenegro said: “I want any Venezuelan watching this to feel empowered, to know that there’s nothing that holds us back. There’s nothing that fucking holds us back,”. The band lit up the stage with a performance that blended caribbean rythms, funk, and amazing energy! Celebrating their identity and bringing it to one of the world's most iconic music festivals.
